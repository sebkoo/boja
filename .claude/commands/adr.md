Create a new ADR:

1. Number = one after the highest in `docs/adr/` (e.g. `0006-...`).
2. Copy `docs/adr/0000-template.md`; title = `$ARGUMENTS`; status: **proposed**.
3. Fill Context / Decision / Alternatives / Consequences — every claim needs a
   link, a test, or a measurement in this repo (per the template).
